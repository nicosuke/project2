A Day in her life

Most of the time She sleeps in a big cage outside the house, she has a favorite corner where she will spend the night. From 8am she and her friend alex will start screaming untill she is released for her morning excersise, which is flying around
in the open sky untill she gets tired. after the excersise she will eat any fruit in front of her, which is normally a banana or an apple.
around lunch time she is put in the outside cage where she will walk, climb and destroy wood. After another afternoon
flight she is brought insde the house to spend quality time with owners for many hours.
Dinner time she will eat human food on top of the dinner table, meat, rice, veges and more.
Afer the meal she will sleep where she is standing, so she has to be shifter to the outside cage or optionally to a smaller cage inside the house.

Story of Pinkchan

Pinkchan came as a baby without feathers, because her parents for some reason desided to take them off. She was hand raised however,
she was a little bit agressive at the begining, so we had to cut her feathers short so she can behave, we did this only once, the first year of her life,
after 6 months her feather regrown and since then she flies happy and free every day.


Habits

Eat, scream, poo, destroy soft toys, chase other parrots, bite most of things, climb to shoulders.
